Diana, Princess of Wales

Queen of people's hearts

Here's a timeline of Princess Diana's life:

  • 1961: Diana Frances Spencer is born at Park House, Sandringham, Northfolk, England.
  • 1975: Diana's parents, John Spencer and Frances Shand Kydd, divorce.
  • 1977: Diana's father become the 8th Earl Spencer, making her Lady Diana Spencer.
  • 1981: Lady Diana Spencer marries Charles, Prince of Wales, at St. Paul's Cathedral in London, in a globally televised ceremony
  • 1982: Diana gives birth to her first son, Prince William Arthur Philip Louis.
  • 1984: Diana gives birth to her second son, Prince Henry (Harry) Charles Albert David.
  • 1986: Diana becomees Patron of several charities and organizations, including Great Ormond Sreet Hospital for Children and the National AIDS trust
  • 1992: Diana and Charles seperate. Diana speaks openly about her struggles in a televised interview.
  • 1995: Diana gives a groundbreaking interview to BBC's Panorama, discussing her marriage, affairs, and struggles with mental health.
  • 1996: Diana and Charles divorce.
  • 1997: Princess Diana dies ina car crash in Paris, alongside her companion, Dodi Fayed, and their driver Henri Paul.
  • 1997: Diana's funeral takes place at Westminster Abbey, attended by dinitaries and millions worldwide.
